About Me

Hello! I'm Umama Saif, an aspiring Full Stack Developer currently pursuing my Bachelor's in Software Engineering at FAST NUCES, Islamabad. I have experience in various programming languages, including Java, C++, Python, C#, and web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

During my academic journey, I have worked on multiple projects like BloodFlowPro, a blood donation management system built using MVC architecture in Java, and a Daycare Management System developed using C# and SQL. I also have experience with UI/UX design and Figma prototypes.

I previously mentored students in the Introduction to ICT lab as a Lab Demonstrator, assisting them with technical challenges and software concepts.

In my Web Engineering course, I learned the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js) and developed several full-stack web applications throughout the course, mainly in lab sessions. These projects helped me gain hands-on experience in building dynamic and responsive web applications, implementing RESTful APIs, handling database operations with MongoDB, and managing state efficiently in React. Through this, I strengthened my understanding of backend development, authentication, and deployment, further enhancing my skills as a full-stack developer.
